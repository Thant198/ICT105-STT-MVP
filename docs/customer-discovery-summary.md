# Customer Discovery Summary

## Project Title
Campus Life - Student Project Management System

## Target Users Studied
- University Students
- Club Members
- Event Organizers
- Lecturers

## Number of Responses / Interviews
10 Student Respondents

## Main Repeated Problem
Students frequently miss important announcements and struggle to track project deadlines because information is spread across multiple communication platforms.

## Main Current Workarounds
- Facebook Groups
- LINE Groups
- Messenger Groups
- Email
- Personal reminders and calendars

## Strongest Evidence Found
- 8 out of 10 students reported missing important announcements at least once.
- 7 out of 10 students reported forgetting project deadlines.
- Most students use multiple applications to manage campus activities and project communication.
- Students reported delays in receiving project updates from team members.

## Weak Evidence or Unclear Findings
- Limited responses from lecturers and event organizers.
- Event-related issues were reported less frequently than announcement and deadline issues.
- Communication preferences varied among respondents.

## What We Must Revise
- Focus more on announcement management and deadline tracking features.
- Gather additional feedback from lecturers and event organizers.
- Validate whether event-related features should be included in the MVP.

## Decision for Next Lab
**Continue with same problem direction**

## Updated Problem Statement
University students experience difficulties managing campus-related information because announcements, project deadlines, and team communication are spread across multiple platforms. This results in missed announcements, forgotten deadlines, poor project coordination, and communication confusion.
