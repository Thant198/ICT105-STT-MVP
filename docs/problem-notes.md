# Problem Notes

## Selected Case

Campus Life – Student Project Management System

## Target Respondents

| Respondent Type     | Why This Respondent Matters                     | Planned Number | Actual Number |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | -------------- | ------------- |
| University Students | Primary users who manage projects and deadlines | 5              | 5             |
| Club Members        | Frequently participate in campus activities     | 3              | 3             |
| Lecturers           | Share announcements and project requirements    | 2              | 2             |

## Observed / Reported Problem

Students often miss important announcements, forget project deadlines, and experience communication issues when working in teams. Information is usually spread across multiple platforms, making it difficult to stay organized.

## Current Alternatives or Workarounds

| Workaround      | Who Uses It?         | Weakness / Pain                               | Evidence Source |
| --------------- | -------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| LINE Groups     | Students             | Important messages are easily buried in chats | Interview       |
| Facebook Groups | Students             | Not everyone checks regularly                 | Interview       |
| Email           | Students & Lecturers | Emails are often ignored or missed            | Interview       |
| Asking Friends  | Students             | Information may be incomplete or delayed      | Interview       |

## Repeated Pain Points

| Pain Point                     | Number of Mentions | Example Evidence                            | Severity |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| Missing announcements          | 7                  | Students did not see important updates      | High     |
| Missing deadlines              | 6                  | Students forgot submission dates            | High     |
| Communication confusion        | 5                  | Team members received different information | Medium   |
| Difficulty finding information | 4                  | Information scattered across platforms      | Medium   |

## Consequences

| Consequence            | Example                              | Evidence Strength |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ----------------- |
| Lower grades           | Missed assignment submissions        | Strong            |
| Poor team coordination | Tasks not completed on time          | Strong            |
| Increased stress       | Students worry about missing updates | Medium            |
| Reduced participation  | Students miss campus activities      | Medium            |

## Notes for MVP Direction

The MVP should provide a centralized platform where students can track project deadlines, receive announcements, manage team communication, and access important campus information in one place. The system should be simple, easy to use, and accessible from mobile devices.
