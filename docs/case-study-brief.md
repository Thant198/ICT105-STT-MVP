# Lab 06 Case Brief

## Case: Campus Life – Student Project Management System

University students often miss important announcements, project deadlines, and campus activities because information is shared across multiple platforms such as LINE, Messenger, Facebook, Google Classroom, and Email. Students also find it difficult to track project tasks, access shared files, and communicate effectively with their team members.

## Target Users

- University students
- Project team members
- Club leaders
- Lecturers / Administrators

## Product Direction

A web-based platform that centralizes campus announcements, project management, deadlines, events, and team collaboration. The system allows students to view announcements, manage project tasks, access shared files, track deadlines, browse campus events, and receive notifications. Administrators can create, edit, approve, and manage system records.

## Lab 06 Focus

Design the business model, technical architecture, data flow, and data structure for the Campus Life Management System. Ensure that all design decisions are supported by the customer discovery evidence, user stories, and MVP features developed in previous labs.
